Match The Memory

Welcome to Vee's World where you'll be tested to see how well you remember. My game starts with all 8 cards facing down, after you pick a card it becomes visible until you chose the next one. If the fruit on the card you selected matches the fruit on the next card you pick the cards stay visible, and if it's wrong it flips back facing down. You have 4 lives if you pair all the fruits correctly you win, if not the game will restart....
